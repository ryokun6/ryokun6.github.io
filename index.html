I’m Ryo Lu, a multidisciplinary designer. Born in China and raised in Canada, I’ve always been curious about design, cultures, and technology. Over the years, I’ve worked on freelance and personal projects from conception to development, interned at large companies, while completing a degree in Computer Science and Biology.

Since 2014, I’ve been solving all kinds of problems at startups. I designed and built SchedulingDirect, an employee management service for businesses, and Pluto, a gamified language-learning app. Sadly both project ended as teams dissolved.

I’m currently leading a design and front-end team at Ping++, a payments company based in Shanghai.
